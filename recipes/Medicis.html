Sandwich Medicis

Description:

That's a sandwich i often do at work, one of our Bestseller.

He's simple, easy to do and you can eat it even hot or cold.

Ingredients:

You'll need to have a few little things !

  • Foccacia bread
  • Vinegar balsamic
  • Rucola pesto
  • Black olive
  • Fresh sliced tomatoes
  • Mortadelle with truffle
  • Mozzarella
  • Rucola salad

The Steps !

  1. Cut the Foccacia bread in half, and spread out the Pesto and the Balsamic on each part.
  2. Add some Black Olive, sliced Tomatoes, Mortadelle and Mozzarella on top of it !
  3. You can melt the mozzarella as well in the Oven, 30s to 1mn at 275Â°.
  4. Finish the sandwich by adding a bunch of Rucola salad
