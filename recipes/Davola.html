Meet the Davola !

Description:

The Davola sandwich is also made in the Foccacia bread.

I made this choice because it's the spicy one of our card.

Ingredients:

  • Foccacia Bread
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Black olive
  • Sliced ham
  • Sliced Ventricina (spicy salami)
  • Thin sliced Cantal
  • Mushroom

The Steps !

  1. Cut the Foccacia in half
  2. Spread out your crushed tomatoes
  3. Add your black olive, ham, ventricina, and your Cantal on top.
  4. You can also put it in the Oven at 275Â° for a minut
  5. Finish it with a bunch of Mushroom and you're ready to go !
